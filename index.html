About

The reporter behind the byline.

Cyrus Farivar is an Oakland-based journalist, author, and educator. For more than two decades, he has covered Silicon Valley, AI, surveillance, criminal justice, and the places where technology collides with public life. He has worked at the San Francisco Standard, Forbes, NBC News, and Ars Technica, and has been published in The New York Times, Wired, Slate, NPR, and elsewhere. He is the author of Habeas Data and The Internet of Elsewhere, teaches journalism at UC Berkeley, and runs Farivar Media LLC.
